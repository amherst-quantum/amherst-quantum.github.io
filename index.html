K-12 Outreach

Inspiring the next generation of quantum scientists and engineers

Quantum Education for Young Minds

AQI is committed to demystifying quantum science for K-12 students across the Pioneer Valley. Through hands-on workshops, interactive demonstrations, and age-appropriate explanations, we bring the fascinating world of quantum mechanics into local classrooms and community centers, inspiring the next generation of quantum scientists.

School Visits

Interactive presentations and quantum demonstrations for middle and high schools

Hands-On Workshops

Practical experiments that make quantum concepts tangible and exciting
